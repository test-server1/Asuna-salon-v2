Facials & Skin Treatments

At Asuno Salon, our expert facials go beyond relaxation—they deeply cleanse, exfoliate, and restore your skin’s natural glow. From Crystal Clear Microdermabrasion to blackhead extractions and gentle, sensitive-skin formulas, each treatment is tailored to your skin’s unique needs in our calming home-based space.

We specialize in advanced techniques that tackle acne, congestion, and dullness while boosting hydration and firmness. Whether you’re preparing for a big event with our instant Mini Lift Mask or seeking long-term skin health, our treatments deliver visible results and a true moment of self-care.

Why Your Skin Loves Our Facials

  • Deep cleansing and Crystal Clear Microdermabrasion to unclog pores

  • Targets acne, blackheads, and uneven skin texture

  • Boosts hydration, firmness, and overall skin elasticity

  • Instant radiance with our tightening Mini Lift Mask

  • Gentle, customized care for all skin types, including sensitive

What is Crystal Clear Microdermabrasion?

Crystal Clear Microdermabrasion is a professional exfoliation treatment using ultra-fine aluminium oxide crystals. It removes dead skin cells and impurities while stimulating new cell growth, leaving your skin visibly smoother, brighter, and more refined.

How do you treat blackheads and acne?

We combine gentle extraction, active ingredients like salicylic acid, and hydrating masks to clear pores, reduce inflammation, and prevent breakouts—especially for oily or acne-prone skin.

Do you have options for sensitive skin?

Yes! We use Crystal Clear products enriched with cucumber extract, Vitamin C & E, and glycerin—perfect for calming, hydrating, and protecting delicate or reactive skin. Every treatment is tailored for your skin’s tolerance and comfort.

What is the Mini Lift Mask?

Our Mini Lift Mask delivers an instant tightening effect while boosting luminosity—perfect before a big event or as a refreshing boost. It smooths fine lines, firms the skin, and enhances your natural glow in just one session.

What does a facial include?

Each facial includes cleansing, exfoliation, steam (if needed), extraction, a targeted mask, and hydration. We tailor every step to your skin’s needs.

Will facials help with breakouts?

Yes. Regular facials help manage acne by keeping pores clear and calming inflammation. We also offer treatments specifically designed for breakout-prone skin.

Can I combine a facial with Japanese head massage?

Yes, you can! Pairing a facial with a Japanese head massage enhances relaxation while boosting circulation for both skin and scalp. It’s a perfect combination for total rejuvenation.
